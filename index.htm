Do-Exp 1

This program is dedicated to the Design of Experiments (DoE) analysis.
It allows simple experimental planning and fast optimization of variables that affect the studied process.





Do-Exp provides users with a full statistical analysis of dependencies of variables (using Student's t-test) and adequacy of equation (using Fisher's test).


Do-Exp 1 supports the following designs:

  • Full Factorial 2k Design
  • Fractional Factorial Design 2k-p
  • Rotatable Central Composite Design
  • Box-Behnken Design
